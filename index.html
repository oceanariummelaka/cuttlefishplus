Cuttlefish Fact


Cuttlefish are referred to as the Chameleons of the sea due to their color-changing abilities. They can do this even though they are completely color blind.
They can even successfully change color to match their surroundings in complete darkness. Cuttlefish are cephalopods, which means they are in the same class as octopus who are also great at camouflage.
